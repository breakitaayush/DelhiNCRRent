    • Flat lets show supply.

    Pick your flow

    The same map supports rent pins, flat lets, and flat hunts. Pick one and post only the basics people need.

    • Flat Hunt is for what you are trying to find.

    Keep the map useful

    The map gets better when people post real numbers and short, useful notes.

    • Contact stays private.
    • Location stays approximate.
    • More real entries means better pricing context.

    Legal

    Delhi Rent policies

    Delhi Rent collects map entries and usage signals, not public personal profiles.

    What we collect

    • `Add Pin` submissions may include area, ZIP code, listing type, BHK, monthly rent, furnishing, gated status, deposit text, building name, metro details, free-text notes, and approximate latitude/longitude hints.
    • Optional contact fields such as email address and phone number may be submitted with `Add Pin` entries.
    • If you use location-aware features like `Near you`, the browser may share approximate device location with your permission.
    • If you use sharing or copy actions, the site may invoke your browser clipboard or WhatsApp link handling.

    Tools and services in use

    • Map rendering and reverse geocoding use Google Maps platform services when configured.
    • `Add Pin` data is stored in Supabase when Supabase is configured for the project.
    • Public map reads use the app’s configured frontend credentials and database access rules.

    What stays private

    • Email addresses and phone numbers are intended to remain non-public and are not shown as part of the public map pin UI.
    • Exact addresses should not be submitted; the product is designed around approximate area-level map context.
    • Sensitive personal information should never be added to open-text fields.

    How data is used today

    • To display public rent pins, search results, area-level context, and live stats based on visible map data.
    • To support approximate map pinning, reverse geocoding, and optional location-based stats.
    • To detect obvious duplicates, reduce spam, and support moderation states such as pending, approved, or flagged.
    • `Flat Hunt` and `Flat Let` entries can be stored and shown on the map when the project is configured correctly.

    Delhi Rent is a community map product. It is not a broker, landlord, or guarantee of quality or availability.

    Use of the platform

    • You may browse the map, search by area or ZIP, view public rent context, and submit pins, flat lets, and flat hunts.
    • You agree not to post false, abusive, manipulative, deceptive, or personally invasive information.
    • You agree not to upload exact addresses, doxxing material, or sensitive personal data in public or free-text fields.

    Community submissions

    • Map information is user submitted and may be incomplete, outdated, unavailable, or inaccurate.
    • You are responsible for independently verifying any rent, availability, property quality, commute, amenities, or ownership claim before acting on it.
    • We may moderate, limit, hide, or remove content that appears duplicated, misleading, unsafe, abusive, or low quality.

    Third-party services

    • The product may rely on third-party tools and infrastructure including Google Maps, Supabase, and browser/device features such as geolocation, clipboard, and external sharing links.
    • Availability, accuracy, and behavior of those services may change independently of Delhi Rent.

    Liability and scope

    • Delhi Rent is offered as an informational and exploratory tool without guarantees.
    • We are not responsible for deals, disputes, fraud, losses, tenancy outcomes, or agreements that happen off-platform.
    • Continued use of the site means you accept these terms as they apply to the product in its current form.
